Kent is a multimedia artist based in Baguio City, Philippines. His experience in editing short films, designing publication materials, and producing other online content started at Saint Louis University - Baguio City under the Humanities and Social Sciences strand during his senior high school. He continued improving these skills at the University of the Philippines Baguio as a graduate of BA Communication, majoring in Broadcast Communication.

As a member of UPB OBRA, UP Baguio's first multimedia organization, he also experienced working with production design and cinematography.

His experiences as a pioneering instructor for the Bachelor of Multimedia Arts program of Saint Louis University has also further advanced his knowledge and skills. Through the program, he has taught courses on writing, color theory, layout design, and video production. He also established the Pagsibol: Multimedia Arts Festival, a yearly event where the BMMA students get to exhibit to their best works.

He is highly exposed to current online trends and news events. He is also open to new ideas and is a good communicator who can collaborate with others effectively.

Outside of work, he likes to play video games and board games, read novels and comics, and document his travels whenever he can.
