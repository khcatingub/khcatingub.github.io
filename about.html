About Me

Hi! My name is Kent, and I'm currently based in Baguio City, Philippines. I'm quite experienced in making short films, analysis and commentary videos, and other online content. Outside of video editing, I have experience writing content for social media. I'm also highly exposed to current online trends and news.

I'm adept at using video and image editing software to produce worthwhile creative projects, and I'm eager to learn how to improve my craft. I'm also a good communicator who can collaborate with others effectively.

Currently, I'm an instructor at Saint Louis University under the Bachelor in Multimedia Arts program. When I'm not working, you can find me playing video games or reading novels and comics.
